About Me

If I could take pictures of one thing for the rest of my life, it'd be construction sites.

I'm a multidisciplinary designer with keen product intuition and manifold execution skills: interaction/visual design, copywriting, and high-fidelity prototyping. Experienced leveraging and extending design systems.

Those I work with have mentioned my:

  • Attention to detail and appreciation for consistency.
  • Ability to engage in both micro and macro-level product thinking, and to smoothly transition between the two.
  • Passion for creating and engaging with a rich, supportive crossfunctional design culture.
